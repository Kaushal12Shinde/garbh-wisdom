ABOUT GRABH WISDOM

At Garbh Wisdom, we guide expecting mothers through a conscious and empowered pregnancy journey.

Founded by Pooja Ji-a certified Prenatal Yoga Teacher & Childbirth Educator-our vision blends ancient Garbh Sanskar practices with modern science to support you physically, emotionally, and spiritually during pregnancy.

We believe that a mother’s thoughts, breath, food, and surroundings shape the baby in the womb. Through curated workshops, yoga practices, rituals, and holistic education, we help you nurture not just a baby—but a soul.

Because pregnancy is not just about birth-it’s about conscious creation.
